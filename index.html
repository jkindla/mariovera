About



Mario Vera is a renound musician and recording artist based in San Antonio, Tx. With over a decade of performance experience, he trancends his music with masterfully written original songs and vocal melodies that are unmatched. Mario performs with his band 'The Black Shades" alongside his close friend and collaborator Matthew Dehoyos. Mario is available for bookings. Contact him today for rates and availabilities.
